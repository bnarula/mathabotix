Welcome to PaperBots

PaperBots STEM Academy is a hands-on STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) Lab focusing on providing Educational Robotics, Coding, Hardware, Software and Game Development classes for K-12 and College Students.

We have our labs in Bengaluru, India and provide after-school classes, summer camps and weekend workshops using our innovative project based learning model in a mentored, fun and safe lab environment.

Program Offerings

Educational Robotics

Educational Robotics is our introductory educational robotics program to get you excited to explore and learn engineering and coding.

Game Design & Development

Game Design helps students explore and learn game design and development while having fun!

Applied Computer Science

Applied CS introduces you to different programming languages and software development skills.
