Why Choose Paperbots

We inspire and create next generation of engineers, scientists and design thinkers by engaging K-12 and beyond in STEM enrichment programs through advanced technology projects. We make technology easy and fun right from kindergarten all the way up to university and beyond. Our curriculum inspired, developed and enhanced in association with university, NSF, University Research We help incorporate STEM / STEAMS learning by creating fun prototypes of real life projects using hardware and software.
